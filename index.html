While I designed and implemented this website primarily using Firefox on Windows, I further tested it on Windows with Chrome and Edge, on Android with Firefox, Chrome and Samsung Internet, and with GNOME Web on Linux ensuring I covered a wide range of the platforms, browser engines and implementations available to me.
